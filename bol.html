Business Course

Business Courses creates value and contributes to all-round progress. It is an integrating discipline that draws on knowledge and skills developed in a variety of areas. Entrepreneurial endeavours are successful when they identify and assess opportunities, and enable action to be taken to pursue the opportunities.

The programme's curriculum blends the traditional components of a management education with the study of those content, skill, and sensitive areas that uniquely define entrepreneurship. Through a combination of academic and clinical experiences, students will develop an appreciation and understanding of the entrepreneurial process.

On completion of the programme, graduates will understand the motivations, behaviours, and strategies necessary to create, implement, and sustain new ideas and ventures.
